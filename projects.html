Project Overview

A landmark 50-story corporate headquarters in downtown requiring sophisticated HVAC systems to serve diverse tenant spaces while maintaining energy efficiency and occupant comfort.

HVAC Systems

  • Central chilled water plant with cooling towers
  • Variable air volume (VAV) systems per floor
